Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how we protect and handle your information.

Last updated: January 20, 2025

Introduction

CardioPredict ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our heart disease risk assessment tool and website.

Health Information Protection

We understand the sensitive nature of health information and have implemented strict measures to protect your data privacy and security.

Information We Collect

Health Assessment Data

When you use our heart disease risk assessment tool, we process the following health information:

  • Age and gender
  • Blood pressure measurements
  • Cholesterol levels
  • Blood sugar levels
  • Heart rate and ECG information
  • Exercise tolerance and symptoms
  • Other cardiovascular risk factors

Technical Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Usage patterns and preferences

Contact Information

If you contact us through our support channels, we may collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Message content and correspondence

How We Use Your Information

Assessment Processing

Health data is processed locally in your browser to generate risk assessments using our machine learning algorithms.

Service Improvement

Anonymized usage data helps us improve the accuracy and user experience of our assessment tool.

We use your information specifically to:

  • Provide heart disease risk assessments
  • Improve our machine learning algorithms
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • Ensure the security and functionality of our service
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

Data Protection & Security

Security Measures

End-to-end encryption
Secure servers
Access controls
Anonymization

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Local browser processing (no server-side health data storage)
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Restricted access to personal information
  • Data minimization practices

Information Sharing

We Do NOT Share Personal Health Information

Your health assessment data is never shared, sold, or distributed to third parties for commercial purposes.

We may share limited, non-health information only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help operate our website (hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or legal process
  • Safety: To protect rights, property, or safety of users or others
  • Business Transfer: In case of merger or acquisition (with same privacy protections)

Your Privacy Rights

Access

Request information about data we have collected about you.

Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.

Deletion

Request deletion of your personal information.

Portability

Request a copy of your data in a portable format.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@cardiopredict.com

Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and tracking technologies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how the site is used
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 18, please contact us immediately.

International Users

Our service is hosted in the United States. If you are accessing our service from outside the US, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the US where our servers are located and our central database is operated.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
