Our Story

CardioPredict was born from a simple yet powerful observation: cardiovascular disease remains the leading cause of death globally, yet many cases are preventable with early detection and intervention.

Founded in 2020 by a team of cardiologists, data scientists, and public health experts, we recognized that traditional cardiovascular risk assessment tools were often complex, expensive, and inaccessible to many who needed them most.

Our breakthrough came when we realized that machine learning could democratize expert-level cardiovascular risk assessment. By training our algorithms on vast datasets of cardiovascular outcomes, we created a tool that could provide accurate, personalized risk assessments in minutes, not hours.

Today, CardioPredict serves hundreds of thousands of users worldwide, helping them understand their cardiovascular risk and take proactive steps toward better heart health.
