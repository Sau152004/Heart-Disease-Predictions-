Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our heart disease risk assessment tool.

CardioPredict is a heart disease risk assessment tool that uses machine learning algorithms to analyze your cardiovascular risk factors. It evaluates 13 different medical parameters including age, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and other cardiac indicators to provide an instant risk assessment.

Our machine learning model achieves 91.4% accuracy based on extensive medical datasets containing thousands of cardiac cases. However, this tool is designed for educational purposes and preliminary screening only. It should never replace professional medical consultation or diagnosis.

Yes! You can use our heart health assessment tool as many times as you need. It's available 24/7 and takes just 2 minutes to complete. This is useful for tracking changes in your health status over time or getting assessments for family members.

CardioPredict works on all modern web browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. It's also fully responsive and works perfectly on mobile devices and tablets. We recommend using the latest version of your browser for the best experience.

No account creation is required! Our tool is designed to be completely anonymous and accessible. Simply visit our website, fill out the assessment form, and get your results instantly. This approach ensures maximum privacy and convenience.

No medical records are required. The assessment uses basic health information that most people know about themselves, such as age, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and family history. If you don't know some values, we recommend consulting with your healthcare provider.

If the assessment indicates high risk, it's important to schedule an appointment with a cardiologist or your primary care physician as soon as possible. The tool provides recommendations including lifestyle modifications, additional testing, and professional medical consultation. Remember, early detection and intervention can significantly improve outcomes.

No, this tool cannot diagnose heart disease. It provides a risk assessment based on statistical models and should be used as a preliminary screening tool only. Actual diagnosis requires comprehensive medical examination, laboratory tests, imaging studies, and professional medical interpretation by qualified healthcare providers.

Yes, your privacy is our top priority. All health data is encrypted during transmission and processing. We do not store personal health information on our servers, and all data is processed locally in your browser. We never share, sell, or distribute personal health information to third parties.

We use minimal, non-invasive cookies only for essential website functionality and performance optimization. We do not track personal health information or create user profiles. Our focus is on providing the assessment tool while maintaining your complete anonymity and privacy.

The complete heart health assessment takes approximately 2 minutes to complete. The form includes 13 medical parameters, and once submitted, you'll receive instant results with detailed recommendations and confidence scores.

The tool is designed for adults and is most accurate for individuals aged 18 and above. The machine learning model was trained on adult cardiac data, so it may not be as accurate for pediatric assessments. For children and adolescents, please consult with a pediatric cardiologist.
